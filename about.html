THE ORIGIN

Founded by SHIVA, an Indian American student straight out the heart of OAKLAND, CALIFORNIA. Growing up in the Town, life was a grind—but the music was the lifeline.

Influenced by the heavy hustle of THE THIZZLER and the polished heat of ON THE RADAR, Shiva saw the campus scene was silent. The underground had no microphone.

Now, TRITON ON THE RADAR is the first university-backed platform making history, putting the campus sound on the global map.
